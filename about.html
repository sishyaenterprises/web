| What we are

Clear snapshot of our identity and values

Sishya Enterprises is a trusted name in commercial kitchen manufacturing, trading, and services based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. We specialize in designing and delivering high-quality kitchen equipment for hotels, restaurants, canteens, and food industries. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner in the industry.

From stainless steel fabrication to complete kitchen setup solutions, we bring expertise, precision, and dedication to every project we undertake.






| Satya Kumar Kushwasha

Founder that shapes our path

With 8 years in stainless-steel manufacturing for commercial kitchens, our Founder Satya Kumar Kushwaha is a mechanical engineer who blends rigorous engineering principles with on‑site practicality to deliver equipment that’s durable, hygienic, and effortless to maintain. Having led cross‑functional teams across design, fabrication, QA, installation, and commissioning, the focus is always on safety, performance, and on‑time delivery. With deep quality assurance expertise for hotels, restaurants, cloud kitchens, and institutional setups, every project follows strict checks for weld integrity, finish, ventilation, gas and electrical compliance, and workflow. Backed by 120 successful deployments performing reliably in live environments, the Founder’s leadership, clear documentation, and precise site coordination ensure kitchens run efficiently from day one.
